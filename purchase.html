To Kill a Mockingbird

by Harper Lee

Genre: Historical Fiction

Available

To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee is a timeless American classic that explores themes of justice, morality, and human empathy through the eyes of a young girl in the racially divided South. Set in the 1930s, the novel follows Atticus Finch, a principled lawyer, as he defends an innocent Black man accused of a terrible crime. Powerful, moving, and deeply thought-provoking, this novel continues to resonate with readers of all ages.
